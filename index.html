Ai Hai, it's Chai Shai!

Welcome to Chai shai! At Chai Shai, we believe that bubble tea should be an experience that is both fun and delicious. Our mission is to bring the unique flavors of South Asian inspired bubble tea to every corner of the world. Our team is passionate about creating unique flavors and combinations that will tantalize your taste buds. We use only the freshest ingredients and take extra care to ensure that our drinks are of the highest quality. We offer a variety of teas, from classic green and black tea to exotic fruit-flavored teas, and our signature bubble tea creations. Our menu also features vegan and sugar-free options. Whether you’re a bubble tea connoisseur or just a fan of something sweet and refreshing, we guarantee you’ll find something to enjoy. Come visit us and experience our unique South Asian inspired bubble tea creations.
